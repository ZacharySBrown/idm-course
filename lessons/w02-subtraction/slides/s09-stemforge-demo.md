# Drop-one-stem — the subtraction ear-training drill

Stemforge recipe `w02-little-by-little-stems` splits `little_by_little.wav` into drums, bass, vocals, and other. We use it to train the ear that Subtraction depends on: *what does this track sound like with one stem gone?*

**A/B listens in this lesson:**

- Full mix vs. drums muted → hear what the kick was doing for the low-end perceived loudness.
- Full mix vs. bass alone → hear how much of the "vibe" was actually one line on one stem.
- Full mix vs. vocal-only → hear the anchor. It is almost never the loudest element.

**What to write in your notebook during the A/B:** *Which stem could I mute for the Reduction section of my own track and still keep the listener?* That question is the Subtraction method, compressed.
