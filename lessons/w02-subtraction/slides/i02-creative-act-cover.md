# *The Creative Act* — the book we cite but do not canonize

Rick Rubin, *The Creative Act: A Way of Being* (Penguin Press, 2023). This is the primary source for the Subtraction thesis quote — *"Perfection is obtained when there's no longer anything to take away"* — which Rubin in turn borrows from Antoine de Saint-Exupéry's *Terre des Hommes* (1939).

**Receipt discipline.** We cite Rubin because it is the source most students will actually read. We flag the Saint-Exupéry lineage in `needs_bib:` so nobody thinks Rubin invented the line. `bib:rick_rubin_creative_act`, quote policy: short quotes only, cite page.

**Deflation.** The book is ninety percent koan and ten percent studio. For the studio ten percent, it pairs well with `Tape Notes #140` and the Jack Conte interviews flagged for purchase.
