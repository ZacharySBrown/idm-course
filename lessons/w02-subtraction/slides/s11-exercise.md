# Ship a 3-minute mp3 in one hour

**The assignment.**

1. Take your most recent 16-bar loop. Not a new one — the last one you made.
2. Write the Subtraction arrangement **on paper**. Six sections. For each, list which elements drop. No DAW yet.
3. Open Ableton. Drop the locators from slide 3. Duplicate the loop across six minutes.
4. One-hour timer. Mute backward. Do not add elements. Do not open a synth.
5. Insert one bar of true silence somewhere between bar 32 and bar 80.
6. Bounce to mp3 at 192 kbps. Three minutes, cut from the middle of the six.
7. Upload before next session. Paper plan in the thread next to the file.

**What we grade.** That the file exists. That is the whole criterion. Handbook §6.1; recipe `w02-little-by-little-stems` for A/B reference.
