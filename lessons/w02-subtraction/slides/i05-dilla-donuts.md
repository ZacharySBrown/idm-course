# J Dilla, *Donuts* (2006) — dynamics arc without a drop

*Donuts* is thirty-one beats in forty-three minutes. Most sit under two minutes. None have a festival drop. And yet the dynamics arc on something like "Stop" or "Workinonit" is unmistakable — sections get denser, sections drop out, a sample mutes for half a bar and returns transformed.

**What this teaches for Subtraction.** Dilla's method on the <term key="mpc3000">MPC3000</term> was pad-level mutes inside a pattern chain. A pad is a track. Muting a pad at bar nine is the Subtraction method, shrunk to a one-minute beat. Charnas 2022, `bib:dilla_time_charnas`. MPC3000 on display at the Smithsonian, `bib:mpc3000_dilla_smithsonian`.

**Transfer.** Whatever you think you need six minutes to say, Dilla said in ninety seconds, with fewer elements, by muting harder.
