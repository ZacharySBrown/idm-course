[pause 300ms] TDE — Top Dawg Entertainment, the label Kendrick Lamar came up on. In-house producers Sounwave — Mark Spears — and DJ Dahi built most of Kendrick's catalog around a single arrangement habit. Call it the *beat switch.*

[pause 200ms] The move — at bar eight, or sixteen, or thirty-two, you replace the entire drum pattern with a new one. No riser beforehand. No crash on the downbeat. No crossfade. The pattern that was playing stops. The new pattern starts. The ear has to catch up. That catching-up *is* the hook.
