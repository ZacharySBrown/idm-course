# Four tracks to rip apart this week

- **Kendrick Lamar — *Money Trees*** (2012). The Beach House *Silver Soul* reverse is the intro; the beat opens up at ~1:55. Switch happens without a riser. `bib:pitchfork_dj_dahi_money_trees`, `bib:whosampled_db`.
- **J Dilla — *Get Dis Money*** (*Welcome 2 Detroit*, 2001). Sample in one mode, Dilla's bass in another. The rub, distilled. `bib:charnas_dilla_time`, `bib:whosampled_db`.
- **Burial — *Archangel*** (*Untrue*, 2007). The 1:30 moment — the kick walks out, the vocal stutters carry the weight. `bib:burial_soundforge_quote` for Bevan's toolchain; the Archangel-specific production quote is in `needs_bib:`.
- **Aphex Twin — *Come to Daddy (Pappy Mix)*** (1997). The *(Mummy Mix)* is the textural cousin. Both are inverse-drop precedents — Aphex arrived first, Burial codified it.
- **How to listen:** play each track with the kick soloed, then with the kick muted. You will hear the arrangement move without it. `bib:rick_rubin_creative_act`, pg 81–93 on listening away from the center.
