# Drum swap — the Ableton walk-through

Two named paths from s04, now as click-by-click.

**Path A — MIDI Recombine → Shuffle.**

1. Select the duplicate's drum MIDI clip.
2. Right-click the clip → **Transform → Recombine**.
3. In the dialog, enable **Shuffle**. Leave Pitch and Velocity off.
4. Click **Transform**. Live rewrites the clip in place.
5. Audition. If the result is too far, Cmd+Z once, re-open Recombine, reduce Shuffle amount. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />

**Path B — Drum Rack hot-swap.**

1. Select the Drum Rack on the duplicate's drum track.
2. Click the **Hot-Swap** button (headphone icon, top-right of the Rack header).
3. Browser filters to Drum Racks. Arrow-key through options while the clip plays.
4. Enter to commit. Cmd+Z to revert.

**Do not tune the new kit.** Week 5 move, not this week. Ship.

**Limitation.** Both paths break flow at the edit point unless bridged. Insert a 1-bar reversed cymbal or a half-beat silence on the bar *before* the duplicate. That sells the transition. Mutation 4 candidates also work for bridges.
