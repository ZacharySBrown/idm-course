# Mr. Bill — the forcing-function producer

Bill Day, Melbourne-based, built a whole YouTube corpus around one idea: commit the loop to audio, delete the source, chop the printed file, repeat. <Citation bib="mr_bill_resample_tutorial" />

His term for it varies across videos — *resample and mutate*, *bounce and chop*, *commit and delete*. Spec A §5.6 standardizes the name. The core loop is the same across three hours of his tutorials: you cannot tweak what no longer exists as MIDI.

The whole Resample-and-Mutate mini-module below is just this loop, written down with screenshots.

Caveat — Mr. Bill's Live sessions are *enormous*. He does not practice the versioning discipline we impose in s12. Follow the method, not the file-count.
