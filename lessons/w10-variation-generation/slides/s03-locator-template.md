# The locator template, before you touch a sound

Spec A §5.2 is explicit: place locators before the first audio plays. Cmd-drop on the Arrangement timeline:

- **0:00** — Intro (texture, filtered drums)
- **0:45** — A section (core beat + one melodic element)
- **2:00** — B section (second layer)
- **3:15** — Reduction (strip to texture)
- **3:45** — Peak (all elements + added chaos)
- **5:00** — Outro (deconstruction, end ~5:45)

<Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />

The locators are suggestions, not contracts. Placing them converts the session from open-ended into finite.

Hebden, Tape Notes 140: *"Arrangement is just everything."* <Citation bib="tape_notes_140_four_tet" />

Your five mutated loops map 1:1 to the sections between Intro and Outro. Intro and Outro are the same loop filtered down to texture. Five mutations, two bookends. Done.
