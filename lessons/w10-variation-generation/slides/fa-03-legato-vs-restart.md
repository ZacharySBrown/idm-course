# Legato vs restart — the phase-preservation switch

Each clip has a **Legato** toggle. It changes what happens at the moment of transition.

**Legato off** — the next clip starts from its own bar 1. Phase resets. Good for drum variants where you want the new pattern to begin cleanly.

**Legato on** — the next clip starts at the *current playhead position within the clip's length*. Phase rides through. This is the setting for pads, long melodic phrases, and any loop where you want the transition to feel like a mode-switch rather than a cut. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />

Legato plus **Launch Mode: Gate** gives you the Autechre move — hold the row change with a foot switch, release, clip restarts. Spec B 12.3 §4. <Citation bib="sos_autechre_april_2004" />

Anti-pattern — legato on across drum variants with different rhythmic anchors. The downbeats slide and you get a phaser-on-kick problem. Legato off for drums, legato on for pads. Default answer.
