# Dave Fridmann at Tarbox Road

Tarbox Road Studios, Cassadaga NY. A converted dairy in the woods. Fridmann built it for Mercury Rev and the Lips, then it became the headquarters for records that refused to be made in LA. <Citation bib="fridmann_davefridmann_com" />

Fridmann's method for *Zaireeka* was ruthless editing under constraint. Each CD had to be listenable alone *and* interlock with the other three. That is a four-way counterpoint problem, not a mix problem.

The transferable move — the constraint decides the arrangement before the arranger does. Our five mutations do the same thing in miniature: the *rule* that you apply exactly one named mutation to each duplicate is the generator. You do not get to pick the mutation or combine two.
