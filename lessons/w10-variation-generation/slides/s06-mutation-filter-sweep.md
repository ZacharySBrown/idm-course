# Mutation 3 — filter sweep across the whole section

**Rule:** one Auto Filter, macro-controlled, across the 16-bar duplicate. Nothing else changes.

**Setup:**

1. Group the whole loop into a **Group Track**.
2. Insert **Auto Filter** on the group. Low-pass 24 dB, Resonance 15%.
3. Map Frequency to Macro 1.
4. Draw a clip envelope on Macro 1 ramping fully closed at bar 1 to fully open at bar 16.

**Unlinked-envelope trick.** Set envelope loop to 7 bars while audio loops at 4. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" /> Sweep never lands in the same place twice — Autechre polymeter, one envelope. Spec A §5.7.

**Limitation.** A filter sweep alone is 2012 festival music. It earns its keep because the section is otherwise identical. You are selling the *return to full bandwidth* on the next downbeat.

Automate one parameter. Not two.
