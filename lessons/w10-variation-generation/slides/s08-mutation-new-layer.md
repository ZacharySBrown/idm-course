# Mutation 5 — new layer that was not in the original

**Rule:** introduce *one* sound absent from the original loop. One.

Spec A §5.4 says one layer and means it. Every new layer costs a mix decision. One = variation; three = a song you did not write.

**Candidates, effort-to-payoff:**

- Held sub-bass on the root. 16 bars, no movement. Weight without activity.
- Two-note ostinato on a new patch — Meld resonator, or Simpler with a field recording. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />
- Reversed texture pad. Warp Texture, Flux 80%.
- Single vocal syllable from Week 9, pitched +7, on beat 1 and beat 9 only.

**Caveat.** The layer must have been *absent* from sections 1–4. If it reappears, it is a re-entry — those belong in the Peak.

**Rule.** Bounce inside the 45-minute time-box. Past 46 = rabbit hole. <Citation bib="rick_rubin_creative_act" />
