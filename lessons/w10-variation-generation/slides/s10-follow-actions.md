# Follow Actions 2.0 — the non-repeating variation pool

Live 12 shipped **Follow Actions 2.0**: per-scene probability, legato, and an **Any** option that mixes a deck of clips non-repeatingly. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />

**Recipe, Spec A §5.7:**

1. Build 8–16 one-bar MIDI variants in one Session track. Small hand-edits — hat drops, ghost snares.
2. Each clip: Follow Action **Any**, Chance **20% Play Again / 60% Other / 20% Any**. Trigger 1 bar.
3. Hit play. Live deals variants.
4. In Arrangement, arm and use **Capture MIDI**.

Committed, non-repeating drum take. The pool is the instrument.

**Stacked with unlinked envelopes.** 4-bar audio, 7-bar envelope on filter cutoff. <Citation bib="sos_autechre_april_2004" /> Autechre polymeter, one checkbox.

**Commit.** **Bounce Groups** (12.3) prints the generative chain as a fixed stem.
