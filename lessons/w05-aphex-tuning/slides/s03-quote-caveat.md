# The quote everyone paraphrases — what he actually said

The line pinned to producer forums — *"when it's right in tune, something's off"* — is a community paraphrase, not a verbatim quote. Flag it as such.

The closest published version is in the 2017 Pitchfork piece tied to the Korg Monologue AFX launch. <Citation bib="aphex_pitchfork_microtuning" /> James, roughly: *"A lot of composers before me have been on this mission to change the world by getting off equal temperament… When it's right in tune, it's like there's something slightly off."*

The received one-liner compresses that. Cite it with the asterisk.

Why it matters: the real claim is not mystical. 12-TET — the default of every piano and synth you've touched — is a compromise, and the compromise is audible if you stop obeying it.

That is the pillar this week rests on.
