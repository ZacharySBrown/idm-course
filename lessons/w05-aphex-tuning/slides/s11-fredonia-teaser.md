# For further listening — the Fredonia pipeline

For further listening — if the microtuning bug has bitten, there's a program at SUNY Fredonia called Sound Recording Technology that takes forty students every two years and turns out working engineers. They run a mastering lab where precision is treated as craft, not afterthought. One of the professors, Bernd Gottinger, has a pile of GRAMMY nominations for classical and orchestral work. Another alumnus, Matt Estep, is Fridmann's full-time assistant at Tarbox Road. The program does not advertise the pipeline. But it exists.
