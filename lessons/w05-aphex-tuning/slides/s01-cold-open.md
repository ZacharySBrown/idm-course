# The confession before we touch a synth

Before we touch a synth this week — a confession. The reason Aphex's drums feel wrong when they're right isn't that he's a wizard. It's that he's been tuning kick drums to the root of the key since 1992.

That's the whole lesson in one sentence. Everything after is the receipt.

Specifically — Selected Ambient Works 85-92. "Xtal." "Ageispolis." The Roland R-8 with the 808 expansion card, running repitched kicks that double as the bassline. No secondary bass part. The kick *is* the bass. <Citation bib="reverbmachine_rdj_gear" />

This week: you do the same, in Ableton, in thirty seconds, with one knob on Simpler.
