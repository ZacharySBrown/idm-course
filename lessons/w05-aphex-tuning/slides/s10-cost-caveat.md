# The cost of tuned drums — what you give up

Every technique has a price. Name this one before you commit. <Citation bib="ableton_live_12_manual" />

- **Modulation gets louder.** Key changes break the tuning. Fix: re-bounce per key change, or Scale-device a live transpose. Both are work.
- **Snare body thins.** Pitching up 5–9 semitones via Classic mode shortens it and raises the formants. Layer an untuned snare at −6 dB underneath for chest.
- **The low end gets crowded.** Kick at E1 + bass at E1 masks. Either sidechain, or drop the kick to E0.
- **Over-tuning kills groove.** Every drum tuned and the ear hears melody, not rhythm. Stop at kick + snare.

Aphex's records sound like Aphex's records because the arrangement earns it. Tuned drums on a weak arrangement sound like tuned drums on a weak arrangement.

Caveat logged. Move on.
