# Ship it — kick to root, snare to fifth, thirty seconds

Tune a kick to the root and a snare to the fifth. Thirty seconds. Bounce. Ship. <Citation bib="stemforge_repo" />

**The deliverable.**

1. Open `live-sets/w05-drum-tuning-demo.als`, or your own session with a kick and snare in a Drum Rack.
2. Pick a root. E minor is fine.
3. First 15 seconds — drums at source pitch.
4. Second 15 seconds — kick at the root, snare at the fifth.
5. Export a 30-second stereo WAV. File name `w05_<yourname>_tuned-ab.wav`.

**The write-up.** One sentence per drum. What appeared in the low end when you detuned — not what you *wanted*, what you *heard*.

**The time box.** Forty-five minutes. If you are still tweaking Transpose at minute 40, you are designing the kick, not shipping the lesson. Commit. Bounce.
