## Preset discipline — Omnisphere as the whole palette

- *Three* (2022). Hebden: **Spectrasonics Omnisphere** is "the synth, main synth plugin I use for absolutely everything." `Tape Notes #140`.
- He browses **alphabetically** — "Plectra Sub," "Swaggering Around." Picks a preset, plays it. No oscillator editing.
- Quote, verbatim: "I pretty much only use presets on things. I don't really mess with... definitely not one of those people that's like, I'm going to start with a sine wave." `Tape Notes #140`.
- Supporting cast: **NI Kontakt** for orchestral (harp on "Daydream Repeat"). **SoundToys MicroShift** for width. **FabFilter Pro-L2** + **iZotope Ozone Maximizer** on the master bus.
- Hardware is a sample-generation loop only: Roland TR-8, Arturia DrumBrute, two cassette four-tracks. Patched briefly, sampled, then shelved.

The rule: pick the preset, commit to the preset, move on. The track is the work, not the patch.
