## Exercise — build the four-layer kick on a Moanin' stem

**Deliverable.** 8-bar loop at 120 BPM over `moanin_glitch.wav`. Four-layer kick stack on its own group. Render stereo master + one stem per layer + notes file naming each layer's one job.

**Steps.**

1. `moanin_glitch.wav` on an audio track. Warp Complex Pro. Tempo 120.
2. MIDI group **KickStack**, four tracks routed to group out.
3. **Main kick** — your favorite kick one-shot in Simpler, Classic mode.
4. **Sub sine** — Operator sine, Ratio 0.5, release 120 ms. Tune to Moanin' root (F1) by ear.
5. **Transient click** — noise burst or hat-attack in Simpler, band-pass 3–6 kHz, 10 ms envelope.
6. **Sub body** — Operator sine, octave below layer 2 (F0), release 300 ms, LP 120 Hz.
7. Solo each. Confirm one job. Sum.
8. Render.

**Constraint.** No EQ on layers beyond the click's band-pass. `Tape Notes #140` — layers are chosen to not fight, not EQed to not fight.
