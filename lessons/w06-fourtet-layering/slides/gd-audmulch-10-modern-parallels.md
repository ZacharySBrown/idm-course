## Modern workflow parallels

What the 2003 AudioMulch pipeline looks like in 2025.

- **Bitwig Studio's Grid** — node-graph modular environment inside a modern DAW. Same paradigm, actively maintained.
- **VCV Rack + Host-FX** — free Eurorack-in-software plus the ability to process external audio through a Rack patch. Closer to AudioMulch than any DAW rack.
- **Reaktor Blocks** — NI's modular environment. Paradigm match, different ergonomics.
- **Pure Data + Jack routing** — the Hebden 2003 workflow runs on 2025 Linux without much change.

For an Ableton-centric producer, the simulation is: **build a heavy Effect Rack, render the source through it, reimport the render, throw away the source session**. That is the pre-arrangement sample factory gesture. What you lose vs AudioMulch is the Metasurface morph per render. What you gain is everything else about using a 2025 DAW.
