## Exercise A — replicate: three presets, 16 bars, no tweaking

**Deliverable.** A 16-bar sketch at any tempo. Three instrument tracks. One Omnisphere preset per track *or* one Ableton Wavetable preset per track if Omnisphere is unavailable. No parameter editing.

**Rules.**

1. Alphabetical browse. First pick that "fits" the chord sketch gets committed.
2. No opening the edit page. No automating macros beyond volume.
3. Mix with Utility + pan only. No EQ on the instrument tracks.
4. Spend the saved time on arrangement — section changes, drops, texture layering.

**Self-check.** If you spent more than five minutes on any individual patch choice, you missed the exercise. The discipline is the *speed of the decision*, not the quality of the patch. `Tape Notes #140`.

Bounce the 16 bars. Save the session named by preset names, not by vibe words.
