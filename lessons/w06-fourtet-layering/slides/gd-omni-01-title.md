## Spectrasonics Omnisphere — the whole palette

Confession first. A gear walkthrough on a software synth feels suspect. Except Hebden names one plugin as *the* instrument, and it is this one. `Tape Notes #140`.

- Released 2008, Omnisphere 2 in 2015, continuing updates through v2.8.
- Developer: Spectrasonics (Eric Persing + team). Ships with ~14,000+ factory patches on current versions. `bib:spectrasonics_omnisphere_docs`.
- Two synthesis engines under one hood — <term key="wavetable">wavetable</term> oscillators and the **STEAM** sample-playback engine, co-driven per patch.
- Hebden's use: preset browser → play. No oscillator editing, no envelope reshaping. The patch is the decision.

The module is not about what Omnisphere can do. It is about what Hebden refuses to do with it.
