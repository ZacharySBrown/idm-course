## Laptop-speaker mixing — the cost

- `Tape Notes #140`: "90% of the listening and mixing and everything for this album… is just the laptop speakers."
- Supplement: Audio-Technica reference cans, Sennheiser HD25s on trains and planes. *Three* released "near-unmastered" by his own account.
- **Upside**: low-end commitment forced by a speaker with no low-end. If it feels heavy on a MacBook, it will feel crushing in a club. The four-layer kick stack solves sub on a system that cannot reproduce sub.
- **Downside** (inline): mid-range decisions land sharper on real monitors. Laptop-only adopters often land shrill mixes on anything bigger. Hebden survives it because arrangement is sparse and the Omnisphere palette is pre-voiced.
- **Adopt**: one laptop-speaker pass per session. **Do not adopt**: laptop speakers as only reference.

Discipline, not a workflow to copy wholesale.
