## *Rounds* (2003) — the *other* Hebden

Reference slide for the Rounds-era module. Domino Records, 6 May 2003. Cover: hand-drawn geometric pattern, black on white — the same design family as *Pause* (2001) and *Everything Ecstatic* (2005).

Tracks referenced:

- **"Unspoken"** — 2.5-hour train ride with the central loop before placing a second element.
- **"She Moves She"** — *Rounds*-era AudioMulch + Cool Edit tool-chain exemplar.

The contrast with *Three* is the pedagogical hook for `s10` and the `gd-audmulch` module. Same artist, twenty-one years apart. Different palette, identical arrangement discipline.

Image note — Domino's original *Rounds* cover art is canonical; avoid streaming-service auto-cropped variants. For *Parallel Jalebi* and *New Energy* single art (referenced across Text Records cycle), Text's official Bandcamp has hi-res masters.
