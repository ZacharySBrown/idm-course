## Annotated tour 3/5 — the FX chain page

Per-layer, per-part, and per-patch effect racks. 58 built-in effects in v2.8.

- **Layer FX** (4 slots) — printed into the layer, so patch-internal.
- **Part FX** (4 slots) — shared across the two layers of a Part.
- **Aux FX** (4 slots) — global sends, shared across Parts.
- Common chain on Hebden-favorite bass patches: tape sim → wide chorus → plate reverb, all pre-baked.

The effects are not optional garnish. They are part of what Hebden is selecting when he picks a preset. A "Plectra Sub" without its built-in stereo imager is a thinner, duller sound. The patch ships with its own mix decisions. `bib:spectrasonics_omnisphere_docs`.
