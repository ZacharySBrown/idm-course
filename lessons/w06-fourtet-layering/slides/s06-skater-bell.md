## Skater — the bell doubling the bass you cannot hear

- `Tape Notes #140`. "Skater" — Hebden doubles an Omnisphere "Plectra Sub" bass with an identical MIDI bell part, pitched up.
- Quote: "You can't really hear the bell too much… without it, it's burying the bass more in the mix."
- The bell is not counter-melody. It plays the bass line transposed up. Reads as a **brightness halo** around the low fundamental, not as a separate voice.
- Same logic on the lead — one melody played on three instruments layered in sequence.
- **Rule.** When a bass disappears in the low-mids, do not automate a high-shelf. Double with a bright voice on the same MIDI, pitched high, centered, 18–24 dB below the lead.
- A/B on headphones: mute the bell, unmute. Bass reads bigger with it.
