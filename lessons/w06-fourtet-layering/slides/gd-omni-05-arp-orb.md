## Annotated tour 4/5 — Arpeggiator + Orb

Two pages that matter for preset-driven producers who do not want to edit oscillators.

- **Arpeggiator page** — per-step pattern editor, 32 steps, per-step pitch / velocity / duration / probability. Many factory "Arps+BPM" patches ship with intricate custom arp patterns that *are* the instrument.
- **Orb** — a circular XY modulation surface. Drag the dot; it modulates pre-assigned parameters per patch. For a non-tweaker, Orb is the entire interactive edit interface.

Hebden has not publicly discussed Orb or the arp page in interviews. But the "Arps+BPM" category is visible in his browser shots. Inference, not receipt — many of the "Three"-era sequencer-feel synth lines could be Omnisphere arp patches played live. Flag as inference.
