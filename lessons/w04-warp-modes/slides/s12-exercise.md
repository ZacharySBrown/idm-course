# Assignment — three modes, three sentences

Take any drum loop. Yours, the Hallogallo stem from `stemforge:w04-hallogallo-warp-ab`, a free break, a loop you bought. One loop.

Render it three times:

1. **Complex Pro** — Formants 100, Envelope default 128
2. **Beats** — Preserve Transients, Transient Envelope 100
3. **Re-Pitch** — unchanged pitch first, then a second render pitched up 5 semitones

Write three sentences. One per mode. Each sentence should answer: what happened to the transient envelope, and what happened to the formant character.

Label the +5 semitone Re-Pitch render `chipmunk_soul_bank.wav` and put it in a folder called `w09-bank/`. Week 9 will ask for it back.

**Deliverable.** Four audio files plus a three-sentence text note, zipped as `w04-<yourname>-warp-ab.zip`, uploaded by the deadline. Thirty to sixty minutes, no longer. If you spend ninety, you are over-thinking it.
