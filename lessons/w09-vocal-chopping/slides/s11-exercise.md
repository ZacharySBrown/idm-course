# Three methods, one phrase, three exports

Source: stemforge Hot Pants vocal isolate, recipe `w09-hot-pants-vocal-stem` (`bib:stemforge_repo`). Pick a **four-bar phrase** and work only on that phrase.

Three bounces, one per method. Time-box: 60 min total, 20 per method.

- **(a) Dilla 1/32 micro-chop** — Slice to MIDI at 1/32. Break source order. Nudge 6–8 starts ±15–25 ms per-pad. Bounce `hp_dilla.wav` (`bib:charnas_dilla_time`).
- **(b) Kanye chipmunk +5 Re-Pitch** — Warp Re-Pitch, +5 semitones. Seven non-contiguous pieces. Reassemble into a new four-bar hook. Bounce `hp_chipmunk.wav` (`bib:rbma_just_blaze_lecture`).
- **(c) Prefuse three-pass ladder** — Slice by transient. Finger-drum, bounce, reload, slice, drum, bounce, reload, slice, drum. Bounce `hp_prefuse.wav` (`bib:prefuse_73_xlr8r`).

Deliverable: three WAVs plus one sentence naming the most unrecognizable, and why. That sentence is the whole assignment after the bounces land.
