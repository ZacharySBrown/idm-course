# Vocal chopping: four producers, four methods

- Prefuse 73 chopped an acapella into phonemes and finger-drummed the result — on an MPC2000XL patched into Pro Tools, per XLR8R (`bib:prefuse_73_xlr8r`).
- Dilla sliced vocals at 1/32 and rearranged them until the source was gone — MPC3000, per Charnas 2022 (`bib:charnas_dilla_time`).
- Kanye and Just Blaze pitched soul records up four to seven semitones — varispeed, not time-stretch, per Just Blaze at RBMA (`bib:rbma_just_blaze_lecture`).
- Premier sampled his own scratching and triggered it as a drum (`bib:premier_ten_crack_scratches`).

Same source material. Four different hands on the same stem. This week the stem is the Hot Pants acapella pulled by LALAL (`bib:stemforge_repo`, recipe `w09-hot-pants-vocal-stem`).
