[pause 300ms] The exercise. Three methods, one four-bar phrase, three exports. Sixty minutes total. Twenty per method. The time-box is the constraint.

Source is the stemforge Hot Pants vocal isolate, recipe `w09-hot-pants-vocal-stem`. Pick any four-bar phrase from the bounce. Do not keep shopping for the "best" phrase — the first one you like past bar two of the file is the phrase. Commit. Move.

[pause 300ms]

Method A. Dilla one-thirty-second micro-chop rearrangement. Slice to new MIDI track, Slice to one-thirty-second. Rearrange so the note order does not match the source order. Run the *Recombine Shuffle* transformation if you need help scrambling. Nudge six to eight note starts in the chains' Simpler instances by plus or minus fifteen to twenty-five milliseconds — different amounts per chain. Bounce as `hp_dilla.wav`. Receipt lives in the Charnas *Dilla Time* biography.

[pause 300ms] Method B. Kanye chipmunk plus-five Re-Pitch. Warp mode Re-Pitch, transpose plus five semitones. Chop into seven non-contiguous pieces — vowel, consonant, tail, middle-attack, whatever you pull, just seven, non-sequential. Reassemble into a new four-bar hook. The rule is "make the sample do the hook" from Just Blaze's RBMA lecture. Bounce as `hp_chipmunk.wav`.

[pause 300ms] Method C. Prefuse three-pass resample ladder. Slice by transient — not by grid. Finger-drum a new rhythm. Bounce. Reload the bounce. Slice again. Finger-drum. Bounce. Reload. Slice a third time. Finger-drum. Bounce as `hp_prefuse.wav`. Three passes. Skipping the third pass voids the experiment.

[pause 300ms]

Deliverable. Three WAVs. Plus a single sentence naming which of the three is most unrecognizable, and one concrete reason why. Not a paragraph. One sentence. The sentence is the whole assignment after the bounces land.

[pause 300ms] Prediction, for the record: most students will find the Prefuse ladder produces the most unrecognizable bounce, because each pass compounds the abstraction. Dilla will feel the most musical. Chipmunk will feel the most recognizable. You may disagree. Write the sentence. That is the point.
