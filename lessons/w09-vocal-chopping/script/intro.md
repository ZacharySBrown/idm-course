This week we steal from Prefuse 73, J Dilla, Kanye and Just Blaze, and DJ Premier. Same raw material — a bounced James Brown vocal, isolated by the stemforge pipeline, `w09-hot-pants-vocal-stem`, LALAL backend — four different hands on it.
