# Jenkinson does not want you watching the screen

The thing about Squarepusher is he does not like you — or anyone — watching the screen while he works. That is the whole plot.

Tom Jenkinson, to Vijith Assar in *Tape Op* #89 (2012): *"When the graphical information is too vivid, it makes it harder to retain the information in my memory."* <Citation bib="tape_op_89" /> He sequences on a **Yamaha QY700** — a 1997 hardware sequencer with a tiny LCD and numeric step entry — for exactly this reason. The screen is the enemy of the signal-path mental model.

This week we steal from that posture. Not the QY700 itself — we will simulate it inside Ableton with discipline — but the *refusal*. Single-pass. No undo. Chop the Amen. Commit what you get.
