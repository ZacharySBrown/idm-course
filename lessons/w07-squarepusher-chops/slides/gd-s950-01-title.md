# Akai S950 + Boss DR-660 — the chop system

The <term key="akai_s950">Akai S950</term> (1988) is a 12-bit rack sampler with a two-line LCD and a knob. The <term key="boss_dr660">Boss DR-660</term> (1992) is a compact drum machine with 16 voices, 100 patterns, and a sync-out port. Jenkinson paired them for *Big Loada* (1997): the S950 holds the Amen; the DR-660 triggers it. <Citation bib="tape_op_89" />

Neither box was expensive. Neither was designed for what Jenkinson did with it. That is the point.

The rest of this module is the chain, end-to-end: how the Amen gets into the S950, how the DR-660 drives it, and how the 12-bit companding ADC becomes a signature the way ropes become knots.
