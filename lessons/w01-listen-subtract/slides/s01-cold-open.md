# The course begins with silence

Specifically — the silence after you mute the drums on *Benjamins* and realize the bass was doing most of the work.

This is not a production course. Not yet. This is a listening course that happens to use Ableton.

Twelve weeks from now you will have shipped twelve tracks. Week 1 is the door. You do not open synths. You do not touch Roar. You load a reference track, pull a stem, and write down what is actually happening in the mix.

The rest of the course is built on the assumption that you can do that.
