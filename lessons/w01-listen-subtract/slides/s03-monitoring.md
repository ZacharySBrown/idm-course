# What you listen on matters, and less than you think

The rig sidebar, because it comes up every week:

- **Yamaha HS5** (2014) — the dull, honest near-field. Translates to laptop speakers because it is almost a laptop speaker with a tweeter.
- **Neumann KH80 DSP** (2016) — better near-field, room correction, if you have the room to justify it.
- **AKG K371** (2019) — closed-back, Harman-curve-ish, the cheap tracking headphone most charts agree on.
- **Sennheiser HD600** (1997) — open-back, mid-forward, still the reference your mastering engineer has in the hallway.

Pick one pair of speakers and one pair of headphones. A/B between them every session. The goal is not *flat* — it is *known*. You cannot mix against a surface whose lies you have not memorized.
