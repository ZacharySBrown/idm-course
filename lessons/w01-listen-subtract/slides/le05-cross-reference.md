# Why you cross-reference, not pick one

No surface is neutral. The HS5 hypes 8 kHz. The KH80 is flatter but reveals room problems you would otherwise miss. The K371 has a closed-back low-frequency lift. The HD600 is mid-forward and slightly thin at 80 Hz.

The cross-reference habit:

1. Mix on speakers.
2. Check on the closed-back headphone.
3. Check on the open-back.
4. Check in the car if you have one. Check on AirPods.
5. When four out of five surfaces agree, the mix is probably done.

The studio rat's joke: the mix is done when you have run out of surfaces that disagree. <Citation bib="berklee_online_ompd_382" />
