# Subtract like a producer

Subtraction is the most finish-friendly of the five arrangement methods this course teaches, and the one you can apply *as a listener* before you have a loop of your own.

The move, formally:

1. Identify the densest bar of the track.
2. Treat that as the master state.
3. Every other section is a mute of the master state — intro is everything muted except texture, A adds drums, B adds bass, breakdown drops drums, peak is unmuted, outro inverts the intro.

Week 2 is where you build a track this way. Week 1 is for hearing it in someone else's mix. Play Benjamins and identify which version of the loop the producer treated as master state. Almost always the chorus, rarely the drop, occasionally the second verse. <Citation bib="stemforge_repo" />
