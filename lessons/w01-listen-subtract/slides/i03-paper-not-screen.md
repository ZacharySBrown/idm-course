# Paper, not screen

Booth, in SOS April 2004: *"the screen has to go off. It's an illusion that totally pollutes what you're thinking and what you're listening to."* <Citation bib="sos_autechre_april_2004" />

The notebook is not aesthetic. It's that your eyes go to the meter if they can, and the meter lies about perceived loudness. Hand-write the five axes. Close the laptop lid if you have to.

A kitchen timer on the desk enforces the 30-second chunk. Not a phone — phones pull attention the same way screens do.
