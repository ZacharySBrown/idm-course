# Tape Op and Sound on Sound — the voice-references

Two magazines. Keep them both bookmarked.

**Tape Op** — engineer-to-engineer interviews, light on gear-porn, heavy on workflow. The Squarepusher feature in issue 89 is the template for how this course talks about gear. <Citation bib="tape_op_89" />

**Sound on Sound** — deeper technical breakdowns, the monthly *Inside Track* column that dissects hit records, signal chains with part numbers. The Autechre piece from April 2004 is the origin of the *turn the screen off* protocol this course inherits. <Citation bib="sos_autechre_april_2004" />

Both magazines exist because *Mix* and *EQ Magazine* stopped answering the engineer's question. Read one article from each per week if you want to absorb the tone.
