# Benjamins as the example case

Play Benjamins end to end once and mark which bar you think is the master state. Almost always the chorus. Rarely the drop. Occasionally the second verse, when the producer used the second chorus as the decisive arrangement.

Note it on paper. Compare with your classmate's call. The point is not being right — it is noticing that you can *hear* master state once you are looking for it.
