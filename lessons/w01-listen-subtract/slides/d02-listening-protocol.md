# The listening protocol, as a flowchart

The four-step loop. Run it until the reference and your WIP stop surprising you differently.

The <term key="lufs">LUFS</term> level-match step is the one students skip. Without it you're comparing loudness, not balance — the louder track always wins the A/B. Integrated LUFS on a short-term window, matched within half a dB, is the floor. <Citation bib="berklee_online_ompd_382" />

One sentence per pass. Not an essay. If you can't summarize the chunk in a sentence, you weren't listening for one thing.
