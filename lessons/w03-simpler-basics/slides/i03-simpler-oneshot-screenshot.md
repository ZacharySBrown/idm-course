# Simpler in One-Shot mode — the stripped-down drum device

The screenshot shows Simpler with One-Shot selected. Notice what is missing from the GUI compared to Classic — no filter, no LFO, no full ADSR envelope.

What replaces them:

- **Trigger / Gate** switch — the lone playback mode control.
- **Fade In** — ramp at the sample start, 0 ms to 4 s range.
- **Fade Out** — ramp at the sample end, 0 ms to 4 s range.
- **Snap** button — pins start/end to the nearest <term key="zero_crossing">zero crossing</term>.
- **Transpose** and **Detune** — for pitching the hit at the device level without relying on keyboard input.

The waveform itself takes up more screen real estate because the envelope and filter sections are hidden. You spend your time here with the Start and End markers, not with a filter cutoff.
