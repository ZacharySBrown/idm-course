# Drum Sampler — per-pad MIDI tools

Live 12 added **Drum Sampler**, a Drum Rack pad device with three pattern-shaping tools built into the pad itself. These run on inbound MIDI, not on audio. The sample stays clean.

- **Velocity Shaper** — redraws incoming velocities against a curve you draw. Low end becomes ghost notes; peaks become accents. Per pad. Kick pads get steep curves; ghost snare pads get gentle ones.
- **Ornament** — adds flams and mordents at a chosen length. 1/64 is the Aphex setting. Turn it on and every snare note becomes a short roll.
- **Time Warp** — bends timing within a bar. Pulls hats late on off-beats, pushes the snare early. Per pad. Dilla time baked into the device.

The sample is untouched. All three operate on the inbound MIDI.
