# Receipts and caveats — what Max actually shipped in Autechre's hands

What is documented vs inferred:

- **Documented (SOS April 2004).** Max/MSP acquired 1997. Navier-Stokes fluid-dynamics patches as modulators. *Confield* is "mostly Max experiments."
- **Documented (WATMM AMAs, 2013 onward).** Booth repeatedly confirms Max as the primary environment, confirms use of custom C externals, references gen~ after its 2012 release. The *"I think the oldest thing is from 2011"* patch-archival quote is attributed widely to the 2018 NTS press cycle — we list the Pitchfork NTS interview in `needs_bib` because the exact wording varies by source.
- **Inferred (community reconstruction).** Specific patch architectures for specific tracks (sim gishel, bladelores, vletrmx21). Published .maxpat files from Autechre do not exist; forum reconstructions are educated guesses.
- **Contested.** The "Booth had to turn the screen off to finish Confield" quote is discussed in slide s04 — the specific tying of screen-off to Confield is community folk transmission, not a verified direct quote.

Write what the manual says. Write what Booth said in print. Flag everything else.
