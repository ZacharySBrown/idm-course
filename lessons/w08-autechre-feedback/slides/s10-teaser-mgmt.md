# Detour — wrongness as signature

Quick detour — because "wrongness as signature" is exactly what MGMT's "Kids" is. In March 2007, at Tarbox, Fridmann tracked the vocal on a Neumann U47 and then assaulted the whole mix with, quote, "substandard 1990s compressors." He never named which ones. The engineers who've tried to reconstruct it on Gearspace have not agreed. The point: the sound is load-bearing, and it came from breaking cheap gear on purpose, and you cannot buy it. You can only imitate the posture.

The connection to this week: Autechre's feedback routing and MGMT's cheap-compressor assault are the same idea at different scales. **Pick a wrong path on purpose and commit to it.** Roar's feedback is your cheap-compressor. Turn it up until the texture becomes signature, then stop turning it up.
