# Elektron Machinedrum + Monomachine — the live rig

Mid-2000s, Autechre stopped touring with laptops. The live rig that replaced them: **Elektron Machinedrum SPS-1UW MKII** on drums, **Elektron Monomachine SFX-60 MKII** on synths, a **Nord Modular G2** for modulation glue, and an **Akai MPC1000** for occasional playback. The whole thing ran without a computer.

The reason this matters for us: the <term key="plock">parameter lock</term> workflow Elektron shipped in these two boxes is the first mass-market implementation of what Autechre were already doing in Max — **procedural variation baked into a step sequencer UI**, no randomness required.

Studio rig was different (Max/MSP in MOTU Digital Performer, Shure Auxpander patchbay) — this deep-dive is the live-era paradigm.
