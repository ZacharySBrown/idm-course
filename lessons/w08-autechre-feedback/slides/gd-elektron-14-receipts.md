# Interview receipts and caveats

The Elektron live rig is extensively photographed but under-interviewed. What is actually on record:

- **SOS April 2004** confirms Max/MSP as the studio environment but predates the Machinedrum-heavy tours. Treat SOS for the *studio* lineage, not for live rig claims.
- **Autechre WATMM AMA 2013** has Booth and Brown discussing live setups in response to fan questions. The Machinedrum + Monomachine + Nord G2 + MPC1000 combo is repeatedly referenced there. Flag: WATMM is a community forum; quotes are paraphrased in thread replies, not transcribed from print.
- **Elektronauts forum reconstructions** of live sets (circa 2010–2015) work backward from video footage to infer patterns and routing. Useful but not canonical.
- **Resident Advisor 2016** feature covers the then-current rig in passing without specific parameter detail.

One honest "unconfirmed" beats three "it is said that." Where a cross-sequencing specific is forum-sourced, flag it inline. Where a p-lock is in the manual, cite the manual.
