# Autechre — generative, not random

Everyone tells you Autechre used Max. Nobody tells you the part that makes the music work: **they refuse to use random operators.**

Sean Booth, WATMM thread, paraphrased from the SOS April 2004 sit-down: *we don't use random operators because every time you run the process it sounds different. How we play the system dictates how the system responds.*

That is the entire week. Not "generative music." Not "algorithmic." **Procedural.** Rule-based, repeatable, composed.
