# Twelve weeks, maybe eight tracks, and a countdown

The twelve weeks are almost over. You have twelve sketches, twelve loops, maybe eight real tracks. The point was never twelve finished records. The point was the reps.

This week you pick two, finish them, and press upload. The rest can wait until Year Two.

Three topics, one exercise. Mastering workflow — LUFS targets, metering, A/B protocol, the true-peak ceiling nobody teaches you. Delivery — Bandcamp versus a distributor, metadata hygiene, file naming. Voice — what actually recurs across your twelve pieces, and what was one-week weather.

<Citation bib="rick_rubin_creative_act" /> Rubin in *The Creative Act*, page uncited here because the exact chapter varies by printing — "the only way to know what you've made is to make the next one." You are at the point where the next one is an EP.

Ship is the verb.
