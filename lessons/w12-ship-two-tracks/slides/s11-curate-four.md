# Curate four from twelve — listen back before you re-master

Before you touch a limiter, do this.

Open a new Live set. Drag every bounce from Weeks 1–11 into the arrangement as audio clips, in order. Level-match them with Utility, the same way you level-matched references in slide s04. Listen to the whole stack, beginning to end, one pass, on headphones.

On paper — this is a pen-and-paper slide — write one sentence per track. Not about what you *meant* to make. About what it *actually sounds like*. Two separate descriptions.

Then circle four tracks that feel most like *you*. The four where your defaults showed up without being forced. These are not necessarily the best-produced. They are the most *recognizable*.

Those four are the EP-candidates for Year Two. You will not master all four this week. You will master two of them — the two that are closest to finished. The other two go into a folder called `year-two-candidates` and wait.

The deliverable for this slide is the text file, not the bounce. Ship the list first. The bounces come in the next slide. <Citation bib="rick_rubin_creative_act" />
