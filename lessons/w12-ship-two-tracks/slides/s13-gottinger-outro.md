# Final note — the Fredonia payoff, then quiet

Final note — we mentioned Fredonia's SRT program back in Week 5. Here is the payoff. Bernd Gottinger, the professor who runs the mastering lab, has engineered GRAMMY-nominated Buffalo Philharmonic recordings of Danielpour, Foss, and Kodály. An orchestra cannot be re-tracked. Mastering it means listening harder than anyone else in the room. The program graduates forty engineers every two years. Most of them ship. That is the whole thing. Ship.
